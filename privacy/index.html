Last updated: April 27, 2026 Effective: April 27, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Hadyn Renfort (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the Up & Down: Golf Bets mobile application (“Up & Down” or the “App”) and related services.

Up & Down is a mobile application that helps groups of golfers track scores, manage gameplay, and record per-hole game outcomes. The App does not process real money or facilitate real-money gambling. All wagering values within the App are abstract scoring tokens (“units”) that have no defined monetary value within the App. Any monetary interpretation of these units is established offline between players and is outside the scope of this Privacy Policy.

By using Up & Down, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Up & Down, you may provide us with the following information:

Account Information

  • Apple Account identifier (provided through Sign in with Apple)
  • Display name (you choose this; it is shown to other players in shared rounds)
  • Email address (provided by Apple if you choose to share it via Sign in with Apple)

Player and Game Information

  • Names of players in your golf rounds (you may add these manually for non-registered participants)
  • Handicap indexes for players (entered manually)
  • Scores, hole-by-hole performance, and gameplay choices
  • Course names and details (which you may enter manually or select from external services)
  • Round outcomes, settlement balances, and per-hole game results
  • Notes, side bet descriptions, and other free-text content you choose to add

Communications

  • Support requests, bug reports, and other correspondence you send to us

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information, including:

Usage Information

  • Features you use within the App and how you interact with them
  • Round and tournament participation history
  • Timestamps of activity
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data (if you have opted into sharing this with Apple)

Device Information

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers as provided by Apple
  • App version and build number

Location Information

The App may request access to your device location for the limited purpose of helping you find nearby golf courses. You can decline or revoke this permission in your device settings at any time. We do not track or store your precise location for any purpose other than course suggestions during round setup. Location data is processed transiently and is not associated with your account or stored after each request.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services, which may share information with us:

  • Apple Sign-In: provides us with your Apple Account identifier and, optionally, your email address. We do not receive your Apple ID password or other Apple Account credentials.
  • Supabase (our backend infrastructure provider): stores the data you create in the App. Supabase processes data on our behalf and is bound by their own privacy practices.
  • Google Places API (when integrated): used to suggest nearby golf courses. Queries are passed through Google’s service; Google’s privacy practices apply to those queries.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

To Provide the Service

  • Authenticating your account and maintaining your sessions
  • Recording and displaying your golf rounds, tournaments, and trip history
  • Calculating scores, settlements, and game outcomes
  • Synchronizing data across your devices
  • Allowing you to share rounds with other players via share codes

To Improve the Service

  • Diagnosing technical issues and crashes
  • Understanding how features are used so we can improve them
  • Developing new features and capabilities

To Communicate With You

  • Responding to support requests
  • Sending important account or service notifications (such as security alerts)
  • Notifying you of material changes to this Privacy Policy or our Terms of Service

To Comply With Legal Obligations

  • Responding to lawful requests from government authorities
  • Protecting our legal rights and the safety of our users
  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the limited circumstances described below.

With Other Users

When you participate in a multi-player round, tournament, or trip, your display name, gameplay choices, and scores are visible to other players in that round. If you share a round via share code, anyone with that code can view the round’s scores and current state in real time.

With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Supabase (database and authentication infrastructure)
  • Apple (Sign-In, App Store distribution, push notifications)
  • Google (Places API, when used for course search)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may not use it for their own purposes beyond providing services to us.

For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to: comply with applicable law or legal process; protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others; investigate fraud or security issues; or respond to government requests.

Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you (via email or in-app notice) before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and gameplay data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal, tax, or security reasons.

Aggregated and de-identified data (data that cannot be linked back to you) may be retained indefinitely for analytical purposes.
