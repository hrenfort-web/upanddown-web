2. Description of Service

Up & Down is a mobile application designed to help groups of golfers track scores, manage gameplay, and record per-hole game outcomes during private golf rounds. The App provides:

  • Tools for tracking scores during golf rounds, tournaments, and multi-day trips
  • A randomization engine that assigns games and team configurations on a per-hole basis
  • Score and outcome tracking for various golf-betting-style games
  • Multi-player support, including read-only viewer access via share codes
  • Settlement summaries that calculate net unit balances among players
  • Historical records of past rounds, tournaments, and trips
